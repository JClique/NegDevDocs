# Black & White Film Developing

## 1. Load the developing tank

Always use the smallest developing tank necessary to develop the rolls of film. 
This minimizes the amount of chemicals used and reduces the risk of overdeveloping the film.

## 2. Mix an prepare the chemicals

Typically, we use ID-11 mixed with water to a 1+1 ratio, however some film stocks and ISO may require different ratios.
